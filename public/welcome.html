Welcome, Agent!

Your Mission with the HTTP Intercept & Decode Agency Begins Now.

You are about to embark on a series of challenges designed to test and enhance your understanding of the fundamental protocols that power the web: HTTP and WebSockets. As a new recruit to 4Geeks, your ability to inspect, debug, and understand web communications is paramount.

What You Will Learn:

  • Core HTTP Concepts: Master requests, responses, headers, methods, status codes, and cookies.
  • Browser Developer Tools: Become proficient in using the Network, Console, and Application tabs to dissect web traffic.
  • API Interaction with Postman: Learn to craft requests, manage authentication (Bearer tokens), and interpret API responses.
  • Debugging Techniques: Identify and resolve common issues in web applications by analyzing HTTP traffic and client-side behavior.
  • Data Location: Understand where to find critical pieces of information within HTTP messages and browser storage.
  • (Coming Soon) WebSocket Essentials: Get a foundational understanding of real-time communication.

Each challenge will present a unique scenario. Your objective is to uncover hidden "flags" by applying your knowledge and the tools provided. Pay close attention to details, think critically, and don't be afraid to experiment.
